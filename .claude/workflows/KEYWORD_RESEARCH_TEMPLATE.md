## Keyword Research Process

### Step 1: Brainstorm Seed Keywords (30 minutes)
- List all your product types
- List common customer questions
- List brand names you carry
- List shoe styles and occasions
- List materials and features

### Step 2: Use Keyword Research Tools (2-3 hours)

**Google Keyword Planner:**
1. Enter seed keywords
2. Export results
3. Filter by monthly search volume (>100)
4. Note keyword difficulty

**AnswerThePublic:**
1. Enter main keyword (e.g., "shoes")
2. Collect question-based keywords
3. Organize by question type (what, how, why, where, when)

**Google Search Console (if site exists):**
1. Go to Performance report
2. Export queries data
3. Identify keywords you're already ranking for
4. Find opportunities to improve ranking (position 11-20)

**Google Autocomplete & Related Searches:**
1. Type seed keywords in Google
2. Note autocomplete suggestions
3. Scroll to "People also ask" section
4. Check "Related searches" at bottom

### Step 3: Competitor Keyword Analysis (2 hours)
Identify 3-5 main competitors and analyze:

**Competitor 1:** [Name]
- Domain: [URL]
- Top ranking keywords: [List top 10-20]
- Content gaps we can fill: [List]

**Competitor 2:** [Name]
- Domain: [URL]
- Top ranking keywords: [List top 10-20]
- Content gaps we can fill: [List]

### Step 4: Keyword Prioritization Matrix

**High Priority (Start Here):**
- Search volume: 500+
- Keyword difficulty: <40
- High business relevance: Direct product/service match
- Search intent: Matches our content goals

**Medium Priority (Phase 2):**
- Search volume: 200-500
- Keyword difficulty: 40-60
- Medium business relevance: Related to products/services
- Search intent: Relevant but not primary focus

**Low Priority (Long-term):**
- Search volume: <200
- Keyword difficulty: 60+
- Lower business relevance: Peripheral topics
- Search intent: Awareness stage only
