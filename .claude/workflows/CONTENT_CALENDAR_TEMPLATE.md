# SEO Content Publication Plan Template

## Publication Calendar Overview

This template helps you plan, track, and execute your SEO content strategy for [YOUR-DOMAIN].

---

## Monthly Content Goals

### Month 1 (Foundation Building)
- **Target:** 8-12 pieces of content
- **Focus:** High-priority keywords, category pages, core product content
- **Content Mix:** 60% product/category pages, 40% blog articles

### Month 2 (Expansion)
- **Target:** 8-10 pieces of content
- **Focus:** Informational content, buying guides
- **Content Mix:** 30% product pages, 70% blog/guide content

### Month 3 (Authority Building)
- **Target:** 8-10 pieces of content
- **Focus:** Pillar content, comprehensive guides
- **Content Mix:** 20% product pages, 80% long-form content

- 2,000-2,500 words: 45%
- 2,500+ words (pillar): 15%
