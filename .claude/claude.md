## Working Principles

### Content Quality Standards
- Original, valuable content (no duplicate/thin content)
- Minimum 800 words for blog posts
- Proper keyword optimization (1-2% density)
- Strong internal linking structure
- Mobile-optimized formatting
- Fast page load times

### SEO Best Practices
- One primary keyword per page
- 3-5 secondary keywords per page
- Natural keyword placement
- Descriptive meta titles and descriptions
- Optimized images with alt text
- Schema markup where applicable
- User-focused content (not just search engines)

### Publishing Standards
- Consistent 2x/week publication schedule
- Quality over quantity
- Each piece must pass SEO checklist
- Track performance of all published content
- Update/refresh content every 6 months
